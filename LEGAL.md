# Legal Notice

## Data Sources and Collection Methodology

All data originates from publicly accessible sources under established legal frameworks.

**Zone File Access**  
Obtained through ICANN CZDS. Access is governed by ICANN's zone data access agreement, which explicitly permits security research and abuse reporting.

**WHOIS Data**  
Queried via RFC 3912 against public WHOIS servers. WHOIS data is designated public by ICANN policy and RAA obligations.

**HTTP and DNS Data**  
HTTP fingerprinting, DNS resolution, and screenshot capture were performed against URLs publicly reachable without authentication.

## Purpose and Intent

This repository supports ICANN Contractual Compliance enforcement, law enforcement referrals (FBI IC3, Europol EC3), brand protection actions, threat intelligence distribution (Spamhaus, URLhaus, ThreatFox), and regulatory referrals.

## No Malicious Intent

This publication documents facts publicly visible before this repository existed. NameSilo, LLC is an ICANN-accredited registrar with public accountability obligations under the RAA. Evidence of non-compliance with those obligations is legitimately subject to public disclosure.

**No non-public systems were accessed. No credentials were tested. No victim data was processed.**

Requests to suppress accurate findings will not be honored.
